Privacy Policy

PD Drug Reference

Anti-Parkinsonian Therapy Guide  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Overview

PD Drug Reference does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The application functions entirely offline.

This privacy policy applies to the PD Drug Reference iOS application developed by Dr Atif Elnil. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users of this application.

Data Collection

PD Drug Reference collects no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No personal information collected
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising or advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No health or medical data from users
  • No crash reporting transmitted externally

How the App Works

PD Drug Reference functions entirely offline. All clinical content — drug profiles, interaction data, non-motor symptom guidance, and the LED calculator — is built directly into the application.

Nothing leaves your device. No internet connection is required to use the app.

Third-Party Services

No third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking services are used in this application.

Medical Disclaimer

This application is intended for use by qualified healthcare professionals — physicians, pharmacists, nurses, and other licensed clinicians. It is for educational and reference purposes only and does not replace clinical judgement, local guidelines, or the BNF. Prescribing decisions remain the responsibility of the treating clinician.

Content is based on NICE NG71, BNF, and SIGN 113. Always verify doses against current local formulary and guidelines.

Children

This application is intended exclusively for qualified healthcare professionals and is not directed at or intended for use by children under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. As no data is collected, changes will be minimal and informational only.
